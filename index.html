Hello! I'm Hari Krishnan

I’m a graduate student at University of California Los Angeles, pursuing my masters in Mechanical Engineering, focusing on Design and Robotics.Inter Disciplinary research have always been my passion. As a part of my internships and projects, I have worked in areas ranging from Mechanical Design, Prototyping, Electronics, Control Systems and even Image Processing. I believe that simple and efficient designs are always the hardest to create and I strive towards developing affordable, robust and user-centric devices. I am currently pursuing my research in the field of bio inspired bipedal robots and multi appendage robotic systems. Apart from my research, I work on couple of social projects, tinker with Photoshop and love discussing about the latest gadgets

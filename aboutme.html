About Me

I'm a graduate student at University of California Los Angeles, pursuing my masters in Mechanical Engineering, focusing on Design and Robotics.Inter Disciplinary research have always been my passion. As a part of my internships and projects, I have worked in areas ranging from Mechanical Design, Prototyping, Electronics, Control Systems and even Image Processing.I believe that simple and efficient designs are always the hardest to create and I strive towards developing affordable, robust and user-centric devices. I am currently working at the Robotics and Mechanisms Laboratory (RoMeLa) at UCLA under the guidance of Dr.Dennis Hong. I am involved in the Design and Analysis of our Hexapod Robot and several other smaller project involving Robotic simulation with LabVIEW, component design etc.
Specific areas of interest and previous experiences have been in -
●Robotics and autonomous systems.
●Design and prototyping of various devices.
●Fabrication and testing of Electro Mechanical systems.
●Rapid Prototyping
●Virtual instrumentation and programming using LabVIEW
●Manipulator systems.
●Modelling and simulation of systems using MATLAB - SIMULINK environment
Classes : Linear Dynamic Systems, Robotics, Compliant Mechanisms, Rapid prototyping.
Apart from my research, I work on couple of social projects, tinker with Photoshop, talk a lot about start ups and love discussing about the latest gadgets.

Rotaract NITK

Rotaract Club of NITK is one of the five exclusive clubs of the institute as well as the sole socio-cultural club. Having its accreditation from Rotary International, established in 1962, Rotaract Clubs (or “Rotary in Action” as they call it) reach international standards.

Whispering Winds

Whispering winds is an international newsletter , released every month among various Rotaract club around the world. Initiated by the Rotaract Club of NITK I have been the lead designer and have been responsible to put together content and design the entire layout of various issues.

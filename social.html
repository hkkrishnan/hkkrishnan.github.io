The Ooperai Project

OOPERAI is philanthropic foundation for built environment awareness at early ages.In 2011, a foundation called OOperai was created in Panama with the scope to give accessibility to a creative and evaluative training of the built environment at early ages. In 2013, OOperai started a project in India with children from Chennai and Mumbai, where we are implementing more creative engagements and exchanging their issues regularly. Annual Charity Play | Rotaract NITK

Every aspect of the play , right from the directing, acting , set design , marketing etc ,is completely handled by the students themselves and it also provides a platform for the students to showcase their acting skills.
